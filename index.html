What is Flag Football?

Flag Football is a fun, safe, and non-contact version of American football!

Every player wears a belt with two flags. Defenders "tackle" by gently pulling a flag — no hitting, no pushing, no falling!

The ball can only be thrown forward. The goal is to get the ball into the opponent's end zone to score 6 points!

Common Fouls

Flag Guarding

Using your hands to block your own flag so others can't pull it.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

Pushing / Illegal Blocking

Pushing defenders with your body or blocking illegally.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

How to Play with 5 Players

Offense (Attacking Team)

  • 🐻 Quarterback (QB) — Throws the ball to teammates
  • 🐒 Wide Receiver (WR) — Runs fast and catches the ball
  • 🐴 Running Back (RB) — Runs with the ball after handoff

Defense (Defending Team)

  • 🐆 Rusher — Rushes the quarterback to pull the flag
  • 🦒 Defensive Back / Safety — Covers receivers and stops long passes
Everyone should try every position! This is how you become a great all-round player! 🏆
