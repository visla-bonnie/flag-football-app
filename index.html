What is Flag Football?

Flag Football is a fun, safe, non-contact version of American football.

Players wear belts with two flags. Defenders tackle by pulling a flag — no hitting or pushing!

The ball is thrown forward. Score 6 points by reaching the end zone!

Common Fouls

Flag Guarding

Blocking your own flag with your hands.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

Pushing / Illegal Blocking

Pushing other players with your body.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

How to Play with 5 Players

Offense

  • 🐻 Quarterback (QB) — Throws the ball
  • 🐒 Wide Receiver (WR) — Catches the ball
  • 🐴 Running Back (RB) — Runs with the ball

Defense

  • 🐆 Rusher — Rushes the QB to pull flag
  • 🦒 Defensive Back — Covers receivers
