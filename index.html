What is Flag Football?

Flag Football is a safe, non-contact version of American football. Players wear flag belts. Defenders pull the flag instead of tackling. The goal is to score touchdowns by reaching the end zone.

Common Fouls

Flag Guarding

Blocking your own flag with hands.

Penalty: 10 yards

Pushing / Blocking

Using body to push or block illegally.

Penalty: 10 yards

How to Play with 5 Players

Offense

  • 🐻 Quarterback (QB) - Throws the ball
  • 🐒 Wide Receiver (WR) - Catches the ball
  • 🐴 Running Back (RB) - Runs with the ball

Defense

  • 🐆 Rusher - Pulls QB's flag
  • 🦒 Defensive Back - Covers receivers
