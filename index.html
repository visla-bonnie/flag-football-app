What is Flag Football?

Flag football is a safe, non-contact version of American football. Instead of tackling, defenders pull a player’s flag belt to stop the play. Teams try to move the ball down the field and score a touchdown in the end zone. 夺旗橄榄球是美式橄榄球的安全版，不用擒抱，而是通过拉旗来停止进攻。

🏈
What players do / 球员做什么
Throw, catch, run, defend, and pull flags. 传球、接球、跑动、防守、拉旗。
🚩
How a play stops / 何时停止
A flag gets pulled, the pass is incomplete, or a score happens. 拉旗、传球未完成或得分。
🧠
What kids learn / 学到什么
Teamwork, spacing, passing timing, and sportsmanship. 团队合作、站位、传球时机和体育精神。
😊
Why it’s fun / 为什么好玩
Fast plays, safer rules, and everyone can join. 节奏快、更安全、人人都能参与。

Simple game flow / 简单流程

1. Start the play / 开球
The center snaps the ball to the quarterback. 中锋把球传给四分卫。
2. Choose a move / 选择战术
The offense can pass, hand off, or run. 进攻方可以传球、交接球或自己跑。
3. Defend safely / 安全防守
The defense covers players and tries to pull the flag. 防守方盯防并尝试拉旗。
4. Score / 得分
Reach the end zone for a touchdown. 跑到达阵区就得分。

Common Fouls / 常见犯规

These are kid-friendly teaching examples. Exact penalties can change by league. 这些是适合课堂教学的示例，不同学校或联赛的处罚距离可能不同。

🚫🖐️
Flag Guarding / 护旗
Using your hands or the ball to stop someone from pulling your flag. 用手或球挡住别人拉旗。
🚫💥
Illegal Contact / 非法身体接触
Pushing, tackling, or rough contact. 推人、擒抱或粗暴碰撞。
🚫⏱️
Offside / 越位
Crossing the line too early before the snap. 开球前太早越过开球线。
🚫🌀
Dangerous Spin / 危险转身
Spinning or jumping into contact unsafely. 不安全地转身或跳向对手。
🚫🤾
Illegal Block / 非法阻挡
Using your body to block when the rule does not allow it. 规则不允许时用身体阻挡。
🚫🎯
Pass Interference / 传球干扰
Grabbing or bumping a receiver too early. 球还没到就拉拽或撞击接球员。
