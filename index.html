What is Flag Football?

Flag football is a safe, non-contact version of American football. Instead of tackling, defenders pull a player’s flag belt to stop the play. Teams try to move the ball down the field and score a touchdown in the end zone. 夺旗橄榄球是美式橄榄球的安全版，不用擒抱，而是通过拉旗来停止进攻。

Simple game flow / 简单流程

1. Start the play / 开球
The center snaps the ball to the quarterback. 中锋把球传给四分卫。
2. Choose a move / 选择战术
The offense can pass, hand off, or run. 进攻方可以传球、交接球或自己跑。
3. Defend safely / 安全防守
The defense covers players and tries to pull the flag. 防守方盯防并尝试拉旗。
4. Score / 得分
Reach the end zone for a touchdown. 跑到达阵区就得分。

Flag Guarding / 护旗

Using hands, arms, or the ball to stop someone from pulling your flag. 用手、手臂或球挡住别人拉旗。

Why it is a foul / 为什么犯规

Flag football is supposed to stop the play with a clean flag pull, not with blocking or hiding the flag. 夺旗橄榄球应该通过干净地拉旗来停止进攻，而不是靠挡住或藏起旗子。

Kid tip / 给孩子的提醒

Keep your hands free and let the defender try to grab the flag fairly. 手要自然摆动，让防守者公平地尝试拉旗。

Quarterback / 四分卫

The quarterback gets the snap and decides whether to throw, hand off, or run. 四分卫接球后决定是传球、交接球还是自己跑。

Main job / 主要任务

Lead the offense and make quick smart choices. 带领进攻并快速做出聪明的决定。

What kids should remember / 孩子要记住

Look up, stay calm, and know where teammates are moving. 抬头观察、保持冷静，并知道队友往哪里跑。
