What is Flag Football?

Flag Football is a super safe, non-contact version of American football!

Each player wears a belt with two flags. Defenders just pull the flag instead of tackling. No hitting, no pushing, no falling down!

🏈 The ball can only be thrown forward. Goal: Run or pass the ball into the opponent's end zone to score 6 points!

Common Fouls (Remember These!)

Flag Guarding

Using hands to block your own flag so others can't pull it.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

Pushing / Illegal Blocking

Pushing defenders with your body.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

How to Play with 5 Players

Offense (Attacking Team)

  • 🐻 Quarterback (QB) — Throws the ball
  • 🐒 Wide Receiver (WR) — Runs and catches the ball
  • 🐴 Running Back (RB) — Runs with the ball

Defense (Defending Team)

  • 🐆 Rusher — Rushes the QB to pull flag
  • 🦒 Defensive Back / Safety — Covers receivers and prevents long passes

Everyone should try every position! That's how you become a great player! 🏆
