What is Flag Football?

Flag football is a safe, non-contact version of American football. Instead of tackling, defenders pull a player’s flag belt to stop the play. Teams try to move the ball down the field and score a touchdown in the end zone. 夺旗橄榄球是美式橄榄球的安全版，不用擒抱，而是通过拉旗来停止进攻。

Simple game flow / 简单流程

1. Start the play / 开球
The center snaps the ball to the quarterback. 中锋把球传给四分卫。
2. Choose a move / 选择战术
The offense can pass, hand off, or run. 进攻方可以传球、交接球或自己跑。
3. Defend safely / 安全防守
The defense covers players and tries to pull the flag. 防守方盯防并尝试拉旗。
4. Score / 得分
Reach the end zone for a touchdown. 跑到达阵区就得分。
