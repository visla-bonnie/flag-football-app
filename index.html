What is Flag Football?

Flag Football is a safe, non-contact version of American football. Each player wears a belt with two flags. Defenders tackle by pulling a flag instead of hitting. The goal is to score touchdowns by reaching the end zone!

Common Fouls

Flag Guarding

Using hands to block your own flag so others can't pull it.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

Pushing / Illegal Blocking

Pushing defenders with your body.

Penalty: 10 yards + loss of down

How to Play with 5 Players

Offense

  • 🐻 Quarterback (QB) — Throws the ball
  • 🐒 Wide Receiver (WR) — Catches the ball
  • 🐴 Running Back (RB) — Runs with the ball

Defense

  • 🐆 Rusher — Rushes the QB to pull flag
  • 🦒 Defensive Back — Covers receivers
